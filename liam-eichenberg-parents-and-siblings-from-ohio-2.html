Liam Eichenberg Parents and Siblings from Ohio

Liam Eichenberg parents are Greg Eichenberg and Heather Eichenberg. Liam is the elder brother of footballer Tommy Eichenberg.

Eichenberg is the American Football Offensive Guard. He plays for the Miami Dolphins of the National Football League.

The 24-year-old athlete started his career in 2016 at Notre Dame where he played as a backup for five games.

After taking over the starting left tackle in 2018, the athlete started every game until 2020. Miami Dolphins signed the rising star in July 2021 as a rookie player for four years.

He played in the 2022 season as the starting left tackle but was placed in the injured reserve category until December 2022.

The family of Ohio born are highly supportive of him and his brother’s sports life.

Who are Liam Eichenberg Parents?

Liam Eichenberg was born to Greg and Heather Eichenberg. Eichenberg grew up in Cleveland Ohio.

Liam is the eldest among a brother and a sister.

The Miami Dolphins footballer was born on the day of his parent’s marriage anniversary on January 19, 1998, in Cleveland, Ohio.

His father Greg Eichenberg was a Basketball player at John Carroll University during his prime. Greg was influenced by his father for playing basketball.

The offensive guard was also inclined toward basketball during his childhood which later shifted to football.

The American player’s mother, Heather is a High School Intervention Specialist who is specialized in helping students to make appropriate choices and increase learning habits.

The Rookie’s mother took this approach of setting one or two goals for the future in her home which became helpful for her children in being more committed to their future goals and ambitions.

Heather and Greg played the role of path developers and supporters for the 24-year-old athlete in his journey to the sports life. They have always been there for him to support his decision regarding his career.

The 74-number player’s mom and dad also accompanied him to Notre Dame and Ohio State while choosing between two for future connections after high school.

Liam Eichenberg Siblings

Liam Eichenberg is the oldest of three siblings in his family. Liam has a brother and a sister.

The former All-ACC First-team has a brother Tommy and a sister, Bridget as his siblings.

Bridget Eichenberg was born on 20 January in Cleveland, Ohio. She studied nursing at the University of Cincinnati in the United States.

Bridget is a supporter of mental health and child care. She has kept her personal profile away from the public media and limelight.

But the brother of a former Notre Dame member is known by football lovers as he also belongs to the sports community.

Liam Eichenberg Brother Tommy Eichenberg

Tommy Eichenberg is the junior brother of Liam. Tommy is also a football player at Ohio State.

Tommy was born in 2001 in Cleveland, Ohio, three years later after his brother Liam.

The brother of the Offensive guard is now a member of Ohio State where he plays as the senior Linebacker of the team.

Being active since 2019, the red-shirt player is already a veteran of 18 games and still has eligibility for three seasons.

An OSU Scholar-Athlete is majoring in Business and has already played five games on special teams in his first two seasons.

The semifinalist for the Chuck Bednarik Award first joined the freshman team at St. Ignatius High School as Linebacker and then gradually promoted himself to the middle as a senior.

The American Athlete credits his keenness to play football to his brother who is currently playing for the National Football League. Junior Eichenberg sees his brother as his role model in his sports career.

The Veteran has recorded seven tackles in the wins over Maryland and Michigan State. He was also the Nagurski national player of the week in 2022.

The senior linebacker started every game in the 2022 season on de-fense and had more than seven tackles in most of his games. He was number 1 in the Big Ten with 57 solos as per Ohio State records.

The rising star wishes to make a bigger impact in football through his effort and dedication.

Liam Eichenberg Family Members

Liam Eichenberg has a family of five members including himself.

The supporter of the TAPS Organization rarely talks about his family members in front of the public and media. 

Although having siblings like Tommy, the former St. Ignatius student is not seen frequently exposing his family life in public.

The cousin of the American player, Daniel Finnan is also a high school football player who was in the class of 2020. He is currently studying at Ohio State University.
