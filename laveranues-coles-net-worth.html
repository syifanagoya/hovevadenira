What is Laveranues Coles' Net Worth?

Laveranues Coles is a former American football player who has a net worth of $7.5 Million. Born Laveranues Leon Coles on December 29, 1977, in Jacksonville, Florida, he served as wide receiver for eleven seasons in the National Football League (NFL). Since Coles' childhood nickname was "Trouble", he was often suspended while playing college football for Florida State University. After graduation, he was drafted by the New York Jets in the third round of the 2000 NFL Draft.

It was on October 23, 2000 that Coles made his first career touchdown catch in the Jets' "Monday Night Miracle" victory against the Miami Dolphins. Since, he has embarked on a NFL career that spanned over 10 years. As a pro, he had the chance to play for three NFL teams that go as follows: New York Jets (2000-20002; 2005-2008 and 2010), Washington Redskins (2003 -2004) and Cincinnati Bengals (2009). Even though he started and ended his NFL tenure with the Jets, Coles made the Pro Bowl for the Redskins in 2003.
