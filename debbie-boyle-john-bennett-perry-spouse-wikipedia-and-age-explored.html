During the interview, Matthew revealed that he came close to losing his life in 2018.

He was hospitalized for a perforated bowel, which led to a paralyzed digestive system. After several days in the hospital, he developed pneumonia and fell into a two-week coma, followed by several more months in the hospital. He survived, but the scare had a profound impact on his parents.

Matthew recounted, “One of the things I have to deal with is that my family rushed to the hospital and were told that I have a 2 percent chance to make it through the night. You know, my mom heard that, and my dad heard that.”

A New Beginning

Since his coma, Matthew declared that he is “no longer mired in an impossible battle with drugs and alcohol.” He expressed a desire to move forward with his life and announced his aspiration to become a father.
