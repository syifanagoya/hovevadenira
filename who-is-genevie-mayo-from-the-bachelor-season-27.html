Who Is Genevie Mayo From The Bachelor Season 27?

ABC will begin the new year with a fresh season of The Bachelor. Season 27 of the iconic reality TV dating program is ready to start in a matter of days, making Monday evenings a lot more interesting. This time around, the series will feature 30 women competing for Zach Shallcross’ heart.

The Bachelor season 27 will debut on ABC on January 23, 2023, at 8 p.m. ET/ 7 p.m. CT. Genevie Mayo is one of the 30 ladies hoping to get engaged to Zach before the conclusion of the season. Zach originally appeared on The Bachelorette during season 19, seeking to win the hearts of either Gabby Windey or Rachel Recchia. Unfortunately, his time on the program came to an end when Rachel abandoned him and crushed his heart. Zach, on the other hand, was picked to be the lead in season 27 of The Bachelor because he created a lasting impact.

According to the official summary for the forthcoming season:

“Zach Shallcross’ adventure to find love will continue as he steps in as leading man for the upcoming 27th season of “The Bachelor,” after a painful exit from Gabby and Rachel’s season of “The Bachelorette.”

It goes on to say:

“Shallcross garnered Bachelor Nation’s heart when he made the painful choice to stop his journey on “The Bachelorette” after an emotional discovery about his future with Rachel.” When the season debuts, the charming California native will begin giving out roses.”

With the series’ debut approaching, here’s all you need to know about Genevie Mayo.

Genevie Mayo, a Bachelor participant, works as a neonatal nurse.

Genevie, 26, is a neonatal nurse from Baltimore City, Maryland, according to her ABC profile. Her profile describes her as having a grin that can brighten any room she enters. Genevie aspires to be the CEO of a hospital in addition to becoming a qualified nurse. Her profile covers her hobbies and what she wants in a mate, as well as:

“The boss is on the go!” Genevie enjoys reading Colleen Hoover novels, enjoying sports, and spending time with her family when she is not working. The Maryland native is seeking a partner who is loyal and adventurous, and she only dates in order to discover the real thing. Genevie loves deeply and is seeking the ideal companion with whom to settle down.”

She has slightly over 500 Instagram followers and 100+ Twitter followers as of this writing. However, once the program airs, that number is likely to rise. Keep an eye out to see whether Genevie captures Zach’s heart and how far she progresses on the program.
