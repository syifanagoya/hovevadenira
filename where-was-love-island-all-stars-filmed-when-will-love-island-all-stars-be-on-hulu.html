Where Was Love Island All Stars Filmed? When Will Love Island All Stars Be on Hulu?

The filming location of Love Island: All Stars was in a beautiful villa in South Africa, marking the first time ITV commissioned an All Stars version of this popular reality dating show. The show’s creative director, Mike Spencer, expressed excitement about the series, and mentioned it featuring favorite Islanders from previous seasons returning to the stunning South African villa to pursue love once again. The decision to film in South Africa was confirmed by the show’s producers in an official press release. The luxurious villa, where the filming took place, was the same location where the winners of the previous year’s winter series, Sanam Harrinanan and Kai Fagan, were crowned. The All Stars edition celebrated a decade of Love Island’s successful dating show on television.
