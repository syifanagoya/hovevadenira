John Deere Z930M ZTrak Mower Specs, Price &amp; Features 2022

John Deere Z930M ZTrak Mower Specs: John Deere Z930M ZTrak zero-turn mower powered with 52 cu in. (852cc), Gasoline Engine that produces 25.5 HP power at 3600 rpm.

John Deere Z930M ZTrak Zero Turn Mower features include 7-Iron(TM), PRO deck, Mulch on Demand(TM), Gasoline Engine and Propane Option.

You can choose a 54′” or 60′” Deck, 54’’ Mulch on Delivery, 60’’ Mulch on Desire, and 60’’ Mulch On Demand 7-Iron II Pro deck. These decks are paired with the 26hp Commercial Kawasaki engines.

John Deere Z930M Zero-Turn Mower also comes standard with ROPS, 11.5-gallon fuel tanks, puncture-resistant front wheels, and ROPS. You can choose from deluxe, standard or suspension seats, just like all John Deere Zorak mowers.

FAQs

What size deck is on a Z930M?

5.5 in.

Blade tip speed is higher than most competitors at 18,500 fpm (5.639 m/min), which allows for more cuts per inch. The 5.5-inch (14 cm) has a deep deck design that allows excellent airflow and material to be processed efficiently and quickly.

Is a John Deere Z930M fuel injected?

The Z930MEFI is powered by an electronic fuel-injected vertical shaft engine, 26.5 horsepower (19.7 kW)

What kind of oil does a John Deere z930m take?

John Deere recommends SAE30 or SAE40 for Z300-series motors. AMSOIL Small Engine Synthetic 10W-30 and 10W-40 can be used in place of straight 30 or 40-weight oils. Deere recommends changing the Z300 Series oil every 50 hours or yearly (whichever comes first).
