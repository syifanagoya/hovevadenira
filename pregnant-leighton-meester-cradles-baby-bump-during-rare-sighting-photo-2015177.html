Pregnant Leighton Meester Cradles Baby Bump During Rare Sighting: Pic

A boy or a Gossip Girl?!

Leighton Meester, who’s pregnant and expecting her first child with husband Adam Brody, was spotted out and about in L.A.’s Sherman Oaks neighborhood on Wednesday, July 15, grabbing a bite to eat with a girlfriend and modeling her fully blossomed baby bump.

The actress, 29, smiled while wearing a white spaghetti-strap top, stretched over her belly, which she teamed with striped pants, Birkenstock sandals, tortoise shades, and a taupe tote.

It was a rare sighting of Meester, who hasn’t made a public appearance since April 2. In fact, both she and Brody, who married back in 2014, have yet to confirm the pregnancy publicly! But there was no hiding that bump when the Gossip Girl alum breezily headed to her lunch date, appearing to be quite at ease.

It’s turning out to be a big time for Gossip Girl babies! While Meester is preparing to welcome her little bundle of joy, her costar Blake Lively had a child—a daughter named James—at the end of last year.
